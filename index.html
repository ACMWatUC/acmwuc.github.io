Hello World! We are

ACM-W @ UC

ACM-W @ UC is a women-interest group for all students who are interested in computing or technology. We host students in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Information Systems, and Information Technology, however, we are open to everyone!

Special Anncouncement
We're looking for an intern!

Want to be a part of ACM-W Leadership? Become an intern for community service! Help the current chairs with new Bearcat Coders registration, processing inquiries, tracking attendances, communicating updates, and improving the program.

Requirements:

  • Be located in Cincinnati
  • Volunteered with Bearcat Coders at least one semester
  • (Preferred) 1st-3rd year

Applications are due Feb 17. Email or DM Liz (tremblea@mail.uc.edu) with any questions!
